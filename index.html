InfeStation
2022 - 2023

InfeStation is an action horror game where you must overcome the odds to make it out alive. Carefully make your way through the space ship and find a way to defend yourself and survive while trying to learn of the fates of your crewmembers. Sneak, blast, and complete puzzles until you make it out alive...or die trying.

-Gameplay programming (All gun logic)
-Level Stream handling
-Tech Art : particles, props, general player feedback.
